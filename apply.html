Apply to be an Exec

Exec Applications open September 11

Greetings.

If you are on this page, we assume you are interested in applying for our Executive Team. As a first year club, there are definitely a LOT of positions open for the taking. And we are definitely excited to have you on our team.

A few important things to keep in mind, all Executive Members will be expected to attend biweekly Executive Meetings. Attendance to the biweekly Workshops is optional (with the exception to Workshop Coordinators), however, highly recommended.

The positions of Graphics and Web Designer are the only ones requiring technical skills. In addition, only Workshop Coordinators are required to have prior experience in Spoken Word. Other than that, no prior experience or knowledge is required for the other positions.

We encourage you to apply for as many positions as interests you. You will also have an opportunity to rank them. However, please keep in mind that applying to positions in different committees (Communications, Events, Finance, Workshops) will require you to attend different interviews, if you so qualify.

Last, but not least, candidates need to officially become a member if they wish to be considered for the positions. Please register through the USC Portal to join SWS before applying.

Below, we've listed the open positions with a short description of duties.

EVENT Director(s): assist with the completion and submission of event proposals; book venues; organize event logistics; coordinate with relevant individuals/groups.

SPONSORSHIP Coordinator: develop lists of potential sponsors; contact sponsors; apply for grants; assist with Finance Committee responsibilities.

WEB Designer(s): maintain this website; upload new contributions; create pages; develop the website.

GRAPHICS Designer(s): design flyers and other related promotional materials.

SOCIAL MEDIA Coordinator: maintain the Facebook, Instagram and potentially Snapchat and YouTube accounts (pages, groups, channels, etc.) for the club; involves making regular posts.

WORKSHOP Coordinator(s): plan, organize and lead writing workshops.
