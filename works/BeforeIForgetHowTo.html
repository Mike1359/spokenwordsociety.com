before I forget how to

Lauren Asner

today
I am allowed
to hurt.
my bed is a
safe place
I am allowed
to stay.
but I can
smell Italian
espresso in the
kitchen & there is a
fresh linen towel
hanging by my shower,
outside there is tender
sunshine and inside
there is always Bon Jovi.
and so
today
I am also allowed
to be okay.
