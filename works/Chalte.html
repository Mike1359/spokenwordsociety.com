(c)halt(e)

[go, walk, proceed]

Aisha Khan

while walking on
his way
while breathing
the same
while laughing
his heart away

he knew not
of her soul

s h a k i n g
and

s h i v e r i n g

she sat alone and quiet
she trembles
and little does he know

that his voice
his presence
from two metres

away
carries the strength

of a mountainous trek
a venture she cannot name

but her heart endures

every

so

often.

he does not see her

she isn’t brave enough

to face him
but she chooses a veil

of all-seeing silence.

his bones continue

but hers falter
his time ripens

yet hers quickens
his mind will proceed

and hers will deny
his soul does search

while hers does cry
until he walks

her moon

and she decides

to halt

his night sky.
